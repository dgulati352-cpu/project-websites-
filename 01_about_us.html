Our Story

What started as a small local clinic has evolved into a sanctuary for eyewear enthusiasts. We believe that glasses are more than just lenses—they are a cultural statement.

Inspired by the rhythmic patterns of Warli Art, our flagship collections celebrate the geometry of ancient Indian tribal life. Every frame tells a story of community, nature, and the human spirit, designed for the modern individual who values depth over trends.
