Privacy Policy

Data Protection & Transparency

Data Collection

At Luxe Optical, your vision and privacy are handled with equal care. We collect personal data necessary to provide professional eye care services, including:

  • Personal identifiers (Name, Email, Phone Number).
  • Prescription data and eye health history.
  • Appointment preferences and styling choices.
  • Payment information for frame and lens procurement.

Usage of Information

The information we gather serves specifically to enhance your experience and health outcomes:

  • To manage and confirm medical appointments.
  • To customize lens manufacturing to your unique prescription.
  • To notify you when your eyewear is ready for collection.
  • To maintain a history of your visual health over time.

Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. Disclosure only occurs with authorized partners such as lens laboratories and insurance providers solely for the fulfillment of your order or claims.

Your Rights

You maintain full control over your data. You may request access to your records, ask for corrections, or request the deletion of your non-medical contact information at any time via our store portal.
